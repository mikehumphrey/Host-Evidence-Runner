Use in Forensic Investigations:
hashdeep is widely used in digital forensics for:
- Generating and comparing cryptographic hashes (MD5, SHA1, SHA256, SSDEEP)
- Creating hash lists (known good and known bad databases)
- Integrity verification of collected evidence
- Compliance with forensic standards

This tool is approved for use in authorized forensic investigations.
